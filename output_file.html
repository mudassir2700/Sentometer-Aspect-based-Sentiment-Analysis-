Can I use two 4G (for eg. Jio) SIMs at one time?

Yes, the Redmi Note 8 comes with Dual Standby Dual VoLTE. So you can use two Jio SIM cards or two VoLTE SIM cards at the same time.

Does the phone charge quickly?

Yes, it has support for 18W fast charging (adapter available in the box)

Should I put a screen guard on the screen?

The display comes with Corning Gorilla Glass 5 which protects the display against day to day scratches. You could opt for a screen guard for added protection.

Can I use the rear camera to take portrait photos?

Yes, the quad camera setup of 48MP+8MP+2MP+2MP with a dedicated depth sensor on the rear allows you to take portrait photos.

What is the display resolution?

The screen has a Full HD+ resolution of 2340 x 1080 pixels.
